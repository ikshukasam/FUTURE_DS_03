2. Key Drop-off Insights

Since this dataset contains only the final conversion outcome, intermediate funnel stages are unavailable. However, the analysis reveals several important drop-off patterns:

52.62% of contacted customers did not subscribe to a term deposit.
Customers contacted for a shorter call duration were much less likely to convert.
Customers contacted multiple times during the same campaign generally showed lower conversion rates, indicating diminishing returns.
Contacts with an unknown previous campaign outcome had lower success compared to customers with a successful previous interaction.
Customers contacted through certain communication channels (especially "unknown") showed weaker conversion performance.
3. Actionable Recommendations
Increase Call Quality

Longer and more engaging conversations are strongly associated with higher conversion rates. Sales representatives should focus on understanding customer needs rather than ending calls quickly.

Optimize Contact Frequency

Avoid repeatedly contacting uninterested customers. After several unsuccessful attempts, prioritize new prospects instead.

Improve Customer Segmentation

Target customers who previously responded positively to marketing campaigns, as they have a higher likelihood of conversion.

Enhance Contact Information

Reduce the number of customers with unknown contact details by maintaining accurate customer records.

Use Predictive Targeting

Build a machine learning model to identify customers with the highest probability of subscribing before launching campaigns.

Personalize Marketing Campaigns

Customize offers based on customer demographics, financial status, and previous interactions to improve engagement and conversion.
